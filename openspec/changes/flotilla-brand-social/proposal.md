# Proposal — flotilla brand & social (operator backlog)

## Why

Public relaunch and ongoing socialization need a recognizable visual identity and an owned
social channel. Operator direction (2026-07-03): kraken/octopus mascot (nautical, fleet-themed)
and a dedicated X account with social-media management — with a subtle visual homage to
@firstmates (pleasing aesthetic; do not overdo).

## What Changes

Backlog only — no implementation in this change. Tasks track design and launch sequencing.

## Out of Scope

- Deployment-specific account handles, post copy, or engagement tactics (private operator channel)
- Copying First Mate branding — homage is tonal/visual restraint, not trade dress
